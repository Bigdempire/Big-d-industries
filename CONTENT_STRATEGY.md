## 🎬 Content Types & Formats

### Blog Posts
- Length: 1,000-2,000 words
- Frequency: 2-4 per month
- Topics: Industry trends, how-to, case studies
- CTA: Product links, contact form, newsletter signup

### Videos
- Length: 2-5 minutes
- Types: Product demos, installation guides, testimonials
- Platform: YouTube, social media
- Frequency: 1-2 per month

### Infographics
- Topic: Statistics, processes, comparisons
- Format: PNG/SVG
- Frequency: 1-2 per month
- Distribution: Website, social media, blogs

### Case Studies
- Structure: Problem → Solution → Results
- Length: 1,500-2,500 words
- Frequency: 1 per quarter
- CTA: Contact for similar solution

### Email Newsletters
- Frequency: Bi-weekly or monthly
- Length: 500-800 words
- Content: News, tips, offers, case studies

### Social Media Posts
- Platforms: LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, Instagram
- Frequency: 3-5 posts per week
- Types: Tips, announcements, industry news, customer stories

### Webinars
- Length: 45-60 minutes
- Frequency: 1-2 per quarter
- Topics: Technical training, industry trends, product updates
- Promotion: Email, social media, website

## 🚀 SEO Optimization Checklist

### On-Page SEO
- [ ] Target keyword in title (front-loaded)
- [ ] Meta description (160 characters, includes CTA)
- [ ] H1 tag (one per page, includes keyword)
- [ ] Internal links (2-3 relevant links)
- [ ] Image alt text (descriptive, includes keyword)
- [ ] URL structure (short, descriptive, hyphens)
- [ ] Mobile responsive
- [ ] Page load speed (<3 seconds)

### Technical SEO
- [ ] XML sitemap submitted
- [ ] Robots.txt configured
- [ ] Mobile-first indexing enabled
- [ ] SSL certificate (HTTPS)
- [ ] Structured data markup
- [ ] 404 error pages custom
- [ ] Redirects (301) for old URLs
- [ ] Cache properly configured

### Off-Page SEO
- [ ] Backlinks from authority sites
- [ ] Local business listings
- [ ] Social media presence
- [ ] Brand mentions
- [ ] Press releases
- [ ] Guest posts on industry sites
- [ ] Directory submissions
